Sponsor  Star Lecturers
Why do we have to sponsor 
STAR  LECTURERS?
‘Influencer Marketing’ ;borrow their mouths & images
Focus on key individuals who influence over the potential buyers, especially ATRIX’s target. The Influencers are ‘Star lecturers’ who are successful in their own field. 
By sponsoring these ‘Star lecturers’, Motorola can deliver the message ‘Successful businessman get their power by using ATRIX’ through them to the target customers.
Derivative exposure as a part of lecture 
Many businessmen, ATRIX’s target, are seeking the lecture of these ‘Star lecturers’ and watching it through YouTube, TED or online newspaper. 
It means if a ‘Star lecturer’ uses ATRIX on a lecture use scene of ATRIX will be exposed to the targets through the Internet.
